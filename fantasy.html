Drafting & Scoring Rules

Roster Requirements

Each race week, you must select exactly four drivers within the specified tiers:

  • Tier A (Front Runners): Choose 1 driver.
  • Tier B (Contenders): Choose 2 drivers.
  • Tier C (Dark Horses): Choose 1 driver.

You must also submit a Tiebreaker guess: your prediction of the total number of caution laps run during the main event.

Points Scale

Your weekly fantasy score is the sum of the points scored by each of your 4 drafted drivers. Drivers earn points based on their official race stats from SimRacerHub:

Finishing Position Points Earned
1st Place40 pts
2nd Place35 pts
3rd Place32 pts
4th Place30 pts
5th Place28 pts
6th - 14th PlaceDecrements by 2 pts per position (e.g. 6th = 26, 7th = 24...)
15th Place & Below8 pts (minimum finish points)
Pole Position (Starts 1st)+5 bonus pts
Led Most Laps+5 bonus pts
Clean Race (0 Incidents)+3 bonus pts
DNF (Did Not Finish)-10 penalty pts

Winning the Week & Standings

After each race is completed, driver stats are scraped from SimRacerHub, points are tallied, and the standings are updated.

  • Weekly Winner: The fan with the highest total score. If there is a tie, the fan whose caution lap guess was closest to the actual caution laps is crowned the winner.
  • Championship Leaderboard: A running sum of your points across all scored races. High scores earn you bragging rights and the title of Ultimate Bandit Analyst!
