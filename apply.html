FOLLOW THESE STEPS TO SECURE YOUR SPOT

Because iRacing has removed direct web links to leagues, you must apply directly through the iRacing UI desktop app. Follow the simple 3-step guide below!

1

Open iRacing

Launch the iRacing UI desktop application and log in to your account.

Go to iRacing.com →
2

Navigate to Leagues

On the left-hand navigation menu in the UI, click on the Leagues tab, then click on the Find a League button.

3

Search & Apply

In the search bar, type in our official League ID number:

5234

Click on Bandit Racing League when it appears, and click the Apply to League button!

FINAL STEP: JOIN THE DISCORD

Once you have applied in iRacing, join our Discord server and let an admin know you applied!
